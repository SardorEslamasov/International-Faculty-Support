Arrival Information Form

Important details to guide your first steps in Uzbekistan

A university representative will meet you at the airport upon arrival, holding the university logo for easy identification.
We can assist in arranging temporary accommodation or guide you in booking an Airbnb. Costs typically range from $600 for a single person to $1000 for a family.
You can either book and pay upfront for an Airbnb or opt for temporary housing, paying directly to the landlord with our assistance.
If staying in a hotel or Airbnb, the host will handle temporary registration. For permanent housing, we will assist in updating your registration.
