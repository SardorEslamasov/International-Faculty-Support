Legalization & Recognition of Diplomas

Ensure your foreign diploma is officially recognized in Uzbekistan.

üìå Legalization Process

Step 1: Verify in Home Country

Ensure your diploma is officially verified by relevant authorities in your country.

Step 2: Apostille or Consular Legalization

If your country is in the **Hague Convention**, apply for an apostille. Otherwise, go through consular legalization.

Step 3: Translation

Translate your documents into **Uzbek** or **Russian** by a certified translator.

Step 4: Submit to Ministry

Submit your legalized and translated diploma to the **Ministry of Higher Education of Uzbekistan**.

üìå Recognition of Diplomas in Uzbekistan

Foreign diplomas must be **approved by the State Inspection for Quality Control of Education**.

  • Apply online at TDI (www.tdi.uz)
  • Upload **scanned copies** of your diploma, transcripts, and passport.
  • Pay the **recognition fee**.
  • Processing time: **30-45 days**.

If your diploma has an **apostille**, you don't need additional legalization, but you still need to go through the **recognition process** in Uzbekistan.

It typically takes **30-45 days**, depending on document verification.

Submit it via the **TDI online portal** at www.tdi.uz or visit their office in **Tashkent**.
