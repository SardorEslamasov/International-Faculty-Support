Checklist Overview

To guarantee a seamless transition to your new employment, we have created this checklist. It includes important pre-arrival and arrival procedures and involves multiple university departments. For personalized assistance and a smooth experience, we recommend maintaining contact with our specialists.

Pre-Arrival Checklist

The hiring process can be complex, especially for international faculty members. Before arriving at New Uzbekistan University (NewUU), certain preparatory steps must be completed.

The checklist below provides a structured overview of everything you need to do before and after your arrival.
