Driver License Guide

Steps to Obtain a Driver License

  • Check eligibility requirements
  • Gather necessary documents
  • Pass the written test
  • Complete a driving course
  • Pass the road test
  • Receive your driver license
Step Description Estimated Time
Written Test Multiple-choice exam covering traffic laws 1 hour
Driving Course Practical lessons with a certified instructor 1-2 weeks
Road Test On-road driving exam with an evaluator 30 minutes

Frequently Asked Questions

The minimum age requirement varies by country but is typically 16 or 18.

You typically need identification, proof of residency, and medical certificates.
