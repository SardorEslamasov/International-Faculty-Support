📧 Corporate Email & Personal Pages

Learn how to access faculty resources, academic guidelines, and digital signatures.

📩 Setting Up Your Corporate Email

Faculty members must use their **corporate email** for official communication, accessing the WIKI, and downloading regulations.

1️⃣ Visit mail.newuu.uz

2️⃣ Enter your assigned corporate email & password.

3️⃣ Navigate to your inbox for updates & announcements.

Forgot your password? Reset it via the IT department at it-support@newuu.uz.

📚 Faculty WIKI

The Faculty WIKI provides essential academic resources such as policies, grading systems, and teaching materials.

✅ Log in via **your corporate email** at wiki.newuu.uz.

✅ Navigate to the **Classwork tab** for academic resources.

Ensure your profile is updated in the WIKI for accurate faculty records.

📜 Code of Conduct

The **Code of Conduct** outlines professional responsibilities, dress code, and ethical teaching standards.

Faculty must dress professionally in accordance with the university’s guidelines.

Faculty must maintain **respectful and professional** communication with students and colleagues.

🔏 Electronic Digital Signature (EDS)

EDS is required for signing official documents, including rental agreements.

✅ Visit a Public Service Center in **Mirzo Ulugbek District**.

✅ Submit required documents (passport, visa, PINFL, USB drive).

  • 🔹 Official government portals (my.gov.uz).
  • 🔹 Signing rental agreements.
  • 🔹 Secure document authentication.
