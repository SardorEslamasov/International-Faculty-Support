📱 SIM Card & Phone Registration

📌 Local SIM Card Operators

Uzbekistan has four main mobile operators: Uzmobile, Beeline, Mobiuz, and Ucell. You can buy a SIM card at the **airport** or later from an official store.

🛒 Purchasing a SIM Card

  • ✅ Available at **Tashkent Airport** upon arrival.
  • ✅ **Passport required** for SIM registration (also for family members, except children).
  • ✅ You can either:
    • 📍 **Buy & register immediately** at the airport.
    • 🤝 **Request our assistance** for later registration.

⚠️ Mandatory Phone Registration

📢 Important: The **IMEI registration** process is required by the government for all SIM-supported devices.

  • ✅ **Register your phone ASAP** after buying a SIM card.
  • ✅ Applies to **all SIM-using devices** (phones, tablets, laptops).
  • ✅ Helps track lost or stolen devices and ensures **legal compliance**.

📍 Registration Options

  • 📍 **At the Airport** → Get your SIM & register IMEI at once.
  • 🤝 **Assisted Option** → Our team can help you buy a SIM, and you can register the **IMEI** at an authorized center.

📄 Required Documents

  • 📌 **Passport**
  • 📌 **Visa**
  • 📌 **Purchased SIM card**
  • 📌 **Phone for IMEI registration**

👨‍👩‍👧 Family Members

📢 **Each family member must register their own SIM card** using their passport.

  • ✅ All **phones must be IMEI registered** (including family members' devices).
  • ✅ Assistance available for family members’ SIM purchases.
