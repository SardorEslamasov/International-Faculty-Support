📌 Registration & 🏡 Accommodation

Your guide to settling in Uzbekistan smoothly and legally.

📄 Registration Process

When you move from a hotel or Airbnb to a long-term rental, **your temporary registration is canceled**. You must register **on the same day** you move into your new place.

⚠️ Important: All foreigners **must** register within 3 days of arrival in Uzbekistan.

🔢 PINFL - Personal Identification Number

The **Personal Identification Number of an Individual (PINFL)** is a **14-digit identifier** required for official use in Uzbekistan.

  • ✅ **Mandatory** for all foreign residents.
  • ✅ Required for **banking, healthcare, and legal documents**.
  • ✅ Acts as an **official ID number** like a social security number.

📜 Required Documents for Registration

  • 📌 **Passport**
  • 📌 **Visa**
  • 📌 **Certificate of Registration**
  • 📌 **Personal Presence (Photo & Fingerprints Required)**

🏡 Finding Long-Term Accommodation

  • 🔍 **University Assistance:** The university may help, but private agents are also available.
  • 🕵️ **Real Estate Agents:** Agents charge **50% of one month’s rent** as commission.
  • 💰 **Deposits:** Landlords may ask for a **1-2 month deposit** upfront.

💵 Payment Preferences

Most landlords **prefer cash payments** in **US Dollars**.

  • 📅 Rent is usually **paid once a month**, either at the start or end.
  • 💲 You may negotiate **split payments** (half at the beginning, half later).

🔌 Additional Costs

Rent does **not** include utilities such as:

  • ⚡ **Electricity**
  • 🔥 **Gas**
  • 🚰 **Water**
  • 🌐 **Internet**

Utilities can be **paid via Click or Payme apps** with Personal Account Numbers from the landlord.

🔏 Electronic Digital Signature (EDS)

📌 **What is EDS?**

  • ✅ **Ensures legal validity** of documents.
  • ✅ Used for **rental agreements & official websites** (e.g., my.gov.uz).
  • ✅ Works as a **substitute for OneID**.

📍 **How to Get an EDS?**

  1. 🏢 Visit the **Public Service Center** in Mirzo Ulugbek district.
  2. 🖥 Staff will **guide you** through the process.
  3. 📂 Store your EDS **securely on a USB flash drive**.

📜 Required Documents for EDS

  • 📌 **Passport**
  • 📌 **Visa**
  • 📌 **PINFL (Personal Identification Number)**
  • 📌 **USB Flash Drive** (to store your EDS file securely)
