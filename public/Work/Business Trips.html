Business Trips at New Uzbekistan University

Streamlined information and procedures for planning, booking, and managing business trips.

Trip Details

Essential information for planning your business trip, including guidelines for booking and documentation.

  • Eligibility: Available to faculty, staff, and graduate students engaged in official university business.
  • Destination: Domestic and international destinations related to academic, professional, and research activities.
  • Duration: Typically ranging from 1 to 10 days, depending on the trip purpose.
  • Travel Approvals: Must be approved by the relevant department or faculty head before bookings.

Trip Expenses

Business trip costs vary, but here's an overview of what typically gets covered.

  • Travel: Airfare, train, or other modes of transportation to and from the destination.
  • Accommodation: Hotel costs are covered for the duration of the trip, including any required pre- or post-trip nights.
  • Meals: Daily allowances for meals and incidental expenses during the trip.
  • Other Expenses: Includes conference fees, transportation within the destination, and miscellaneous costs like visa fees.

Booking Process

Follow these steps to book and manage your business trip effectively.

  1. Complete the Business Trip Request Form available on the university website.
  2. Submit the request for approval by the department head or relevant authority.
  3. After approval, proceed with booking flights, hotels, and other necessary arrangements.
  4. Submit all receipts and expense reports post-trip for reimbursement and records.

Travel Guidelines

Ensure smooth travel by adhering to these basic guidelines and university policies.

  • Visa & Documentation: Ensure all necessary travel documents, including visas, are prepared in advance.
  • Health & Safety: Carry the required health insurance, vaccination records, and comply with health regulations for travel.
  • Expenses Tracking: Keep receipts for all expenses during the trip to ensure timely reimbursement.
