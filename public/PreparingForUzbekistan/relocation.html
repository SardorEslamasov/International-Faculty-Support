Relocation to Uzbekistan

Visa Requirements

The A-2 Working Educational Visa is required for academic professionals intending to work in Uzbekistan. This visa is valid for up to 365 days and can be extended. Family members should apply for the PV-2 Visiting Visa, which follows similar extension procedures.

Registration Process

All foreign nationals must register their residence within three days of arrival. Registration can be completed online at emehmon.uz, requiring participation from your host or landlord.

Employment Opportunities

Professors can explore job opportunities on platforms such as hh.uz, rabota.uz, and worka.uz. Foreign citizens may also start their own businesses with tax incentives available for entrepreneurs.

Cost of Living

Uzbekistan offers an affordable cost of living, with reasonable prices for housing, utilities, and fresh produce. Budgeting according to your lifestyle preferences ensures financial stability.

Cultural Adaptation

Uzbek is the official language, but Russian is widely spoken. Learning basic phrases can improve daily interactions. Engaging in local events and respecting traditions will help with cultural adaptation.
