Health Insurance for Family Members

Your family members are eligible for health insurance coverage, providing peace of mind during your stay.

Important Notes

Note: Spouses and children are regarded as family members under the health insurance policy.

How to Obtain Health Insurance for Me and My Family Members?

Health insurance enrollment is an automated process. Once you submit all the required documents—such as family member passports, children's birth certificates, and your marriage certificate—the university will initiate the application on your behalf. The process is quick and hassle-free!

  • Required Documents:
    • Passports of all family members
    • Children's birth certificates
    • Your marriage certificate

Eligibility

All employees of the university who have been granted an A-2 working visa are eligible for health insurance coverage for their family members. This includes:

  • Spouses
  • Children under the age of 18

Family members must be listed in the visa application to be included in the health insurance coverage.

Coverage

The health insurance coverage is designed to ensure that you and your family are fully protected. Please note that the total coverage amount will be shared among all insured family members. Each member will receive an individual insurance card, but the coverage limit remains collective.

Types of Coverage

Health insurance coverage includes a wide range of services to keep you and your family in good health:

  • Emergency Medical Care: 24/7 access to emergency care in hospitals.
  • Outpatient Services: Coverage for doctor visits, consultations, and routine check-ups.
  • Inpatient Services: Coverage for hospital stays, surgeries, and procedures.
  • Prescription Medication: Coverage for medically necessary prescribed medications.
  • Maternity Care: Includes prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care for insured mothers.

Exclusions

There are some medical services that are not covered under the health insurance policy. These include:

  • Cosmetic surgeries
  • Non-medical treatments and procedures
  • Medical expenses incurred outside of Uzbekistan
  • Chronic conditions that existed prior to obtaining insurance

For a full list of exclusions, please refer to the insurance policy document.
