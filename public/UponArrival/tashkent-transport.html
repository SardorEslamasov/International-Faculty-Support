Navigation and Maps

Maps

  • Yandex Maps: Expats accustomed to using Google Maps or Apple Maps may find Yandex Maps to be more detailed for Uzbekistan. It links to the Yandex Go app for ordering taxis and shows the exact locations of buses.

Taxi Services

Yandex Go: Available for both Apple and Android, Yandex Go allows you to order taxis within Tashkent and its surroundings at relatively low costs. The fare depends on distance, car size, and travel time. For instance, a ride from the Tashkent International Airport to Amir Timur Square (Central location) can cost between 20,000 UZS and 40,000 UZS (1.50 - 3.50 USD). Payment options include cash or debit/credit card via the app

Advice:

  • Registration: A local phone number may be required to register with Yandex Go. You can buy SIM cards in many city shops or at the airport. Phones bought abroad need registration within 30 days, which can be done online, at a post office, or at the provider's office, as it might be cheaper.
  • Airport Taxis: Expensive rides are often offered outside the airport. For cheaper Yandex taxis, you can walk a few meters and cross the parking area to order one.

Delivery & Cargo Services

For transporting goods or moving items to a new apartment, the YandexGo or Uklon applications offer cargo services at reasonable prices. The cost depends on the transporter size (starting with the smallest option, a Chevrolet Damas) and any additional services like loading and unloading. Note that some drivers may only accept cash.

Both apps are user friendly, choose the delivery option, manage the car you want, either small delivery for example a box or a whole desk. You can get the help from the driver for an additional cost, set up pick up and drop down, write the recipient's number and send them the link, same with you. You can see where the car is, and how long to wait.
