Visas for Family Members

Important Notes

As you will be obtaining an A-2 working visa, all accompanying family members will be issued a PV-2 visa. For further details, please refer to the Visa Information section.

Visa Details

Flying with You Inviting from Uzbekistan
They will accompany you on obtaining a visa, fly here, and get a PV-2 stamp. Specialists for working with international staff will help you in filing necessary documents.
