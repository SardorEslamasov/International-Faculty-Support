Resignation & Exit Process

Guidelines for international faculty members resigning from New Uzbekistan University.

Resignation Policy

International faculty members must follow the university's resignation process to ensure a smooth transition and compliance with legal requirements.

Notice Period

Faculty members are required to submit a formal resignation letter at least [insert required notice period, e.g., 2 months] before their last working day.

How to Submit a Resignation

  • Upload a formal resignation letter addressed to the HR department.
  • Schedule an exit interview with the HR team.
  • Receive an official acknowledgment of your resignation.

Final Payments & Benefits

Upon resignation, faculty members are entitled to final payments and settlements, which may include:

  • Final salary payment, including any remaining leave balance compensation.
  • End-of-service benefits, if applicable.
  • Tax and pension contributions, if required by law.

Ensure that all financial matters are settled before departure.

Visa & Work Permit Cancellation

International faculty members are responsible for canceling their work permits and residence visas before leaving Uzbekistan.

  • Coordinate with the HR and immigration office for visa cancellation.
  • Ensure you receive official confirmation before departure.
  • Check any restrictions on re-entering Uzbekistan for future work opportunities.

Exit Formalities

Before departure, faculty members must complete the following steps:

  • Return all university property (ID card, office keys, books, laptops, etc.).
  • Submit any pending academic work (grading, research, reports).
  • Ensure all pending dues (housing, utilities, etc.) are cleared.
  • Attend a final exit meeting with HR.
