Leave & Vacation Information

As an international faculty member, understanding our leave policies will help you plan your time effectively. Below are the leave types available to all faculty members at New Uzbekistan University.

Types of Leave:

  • Sick Leave: Paid leave for illness or medical emergencies. Faculty members are entitled to a certain number of days per year.
  • Vacation Leave: Paid leave to recharge and enjoy personal time off. Typically, a certain number of days are allocated per year.
  • Personal Leave: Unpaid leave for personal matters such as family emergencies or significant life events.
  • Unpaid Leave: In case of extended absence beyond vacation or personal leave, unpaid leave may be requested. Approval is subject to university policy.
  • Maternity/Paternity Leave: Paid leave for the birth or adoption of a child. New parents are eligible for a set number of weeks of paid leave, with additional unpaid leave available upon request.
  • Childcare Assistance: Support is available for parents of young children. This may include financial assistance or resources for daycare facilities on or near campus.
  • Public Holidays: National holidays as observed in Uzbekistan, which are also observed by the university.
